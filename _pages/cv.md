# One-Page Overview

## Zane R. Jobe, Ph.D.

With over 20 years of experience in clastic sedimentology, particularly in deep-water depositional systems, I bring a proven track record of integrating static and dynamic subsurface data with outcrop and modern-seafloor data to characterize and predict reservoir architecture and quality. I have led more than 40 field trips for industry and academia, showcasing my ability to translate complex sedimentological concepts into practical insights. My publication record reflects broad recognition in the field at the subject-matter expert (SME) level. I currently manage large-scale, multidisciplinary research projects that deliver applied, business-relevant outcomes to both academic and industry partners.

- Stanford University, Ph.D., Geology, 2010
